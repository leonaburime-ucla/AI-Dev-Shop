# Project Constitution

## Article I — Library-First
Prefer proven libraries over custom infrastructure unless requirements force otherwise.

## Article II — Test-First
Designs must support testability before implementation begins.

## Article III — Simplicity Gate
Default to the simplest architecture that satisfies the real constraints.

## Article IV — Anti-Abstraction Gate
Do not add abstraction layers without a concrete reason.

## Article V — Integration-First Testing
Critical boundaries must have contract or integration coverage.

## Article VI — Security-by-Default
Public surfaces, tenant boundaries, and secret handling must be explicit.

## Article VII — Spec Integrity
Architecture must honor the approved spec and known NFRs.

## Article VIII — Observability
Production behavior must be diagnosable.
